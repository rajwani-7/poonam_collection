## 🚀 How to Use

### Step 1: Start XAMPP
1. Open XAMPP Control Panel
2. Click "Start" for **Apache**
3. Click "Start" for **MySQL**

### Step 2: Setup Database
Open browser and go to:
```
http://localhost/pc/setup.php
```

This will create the database with all 8 men's products!

### Step 3: View Your Website

**Customer Website:**
```
http://localhost/pc/
```

You'll see:
- Men's fashion hero banner
- 4 categories (Kurtas, Sherwanis, Indo-Western, Jackets)
- Product images with "POONAM COLLECTION" watermark
- All content focused on men's ethnic wear

**Admin Panel:**

```
http://localhost/pc/admin/
Username: admin
Password: admin123
```

From admin you can:
- View all 8 products
- Add more men's products

## 🎨 Replacing with Real Photos

When you get real product photos:

1. Go to **Admin Panel** (http://localhost/pc/admin/)
2. Click **"Products"** in sidebar
3. Find a product and click the **camera icon** (Manage Images)
4. Upload your real photos
5. The placeholder will be replaced!

### Recommended Real Image Sizes:
- Product photos: **1000x1000px minimum**
- Category banners: **600x800px**
- Format: **JPG or PNG**
- Max size: **5MB per image**
